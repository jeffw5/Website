Purpose

This instrument assesses the maturity of decision modeling practice across three object types (digital assets, services, products) using a dual-gate framework designed to distinguish AI-assistable domains from those ready for agentic delegation.

Scope

Score against a specific value stream segment or business domain — not the enterprise as a whole. Aggregate scores mask the variance between domains where agency is safe and domains where it is not yet warranted.

How to score

For each question, select the maturity level that most accurately describes the current state. When in doubt, score conservatively. A score of 2 (formalized) requires machine-readable artifacts, not just documentation.

Readiness thresholds

Both gates must reach ≥ 3.0 and decision context must reach ≥ 2.5 for a domain to qualify as agent-ready. High gate scores can be overridden by low context scores.
